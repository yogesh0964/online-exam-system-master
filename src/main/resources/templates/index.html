Features

Everything You Need to Succeed

Powerful tools for students and admins, built to make online examination effortless.

Live Timer

Auto-submitting countdown timer ensures fairness. Never lose your answers — they're saved automatically before time runs out.

Instant Results

Get your score the moment you submit. See what you got right and wrong, and review every question with detailed explanations.

Progress Tracking

Beautiful performance charts show your improvement over time. Track your average score, highest score, and exam history.

Secure & Safe

Spring Security powered authentication keeps your data safe. Role-based access ensures students only see what's meant for them.

Admin Control

Full CRUD for exams and questions. Manage student accounts, reset passwords, view all submissions in one dashboard.

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design works perfectly on any device — take exams on your phone, tablet, or desktop with ease.
